How fast you age depends on how fast your blood sugar spikes. Energy, bloating, brain fog and weight gain are all tied to blood sugar. So is aging velocity. Every glucose spike is a micro-hit of oxidative stress and inflammation—tiny but cumulative wear and tear at the cellular level. When glucose rises quickly, it fuels the formation of advanced glycation end products (AGEs)—molecules that stiffen blood vessels, break down collagen, impair mitochondria, and disrupt DNA repair.
