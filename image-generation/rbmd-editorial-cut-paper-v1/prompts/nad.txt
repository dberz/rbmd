Food Comes First: Tryptophan-rich proteins and B3 sources like quinoa, legumes, and leafy greens help your body produce NAD+ before supplements are needed. Injection Gaps Remain: Injected NAD+ bypasses digestion for direct cellular uptake, but how much is absorbed and by which cells is still poorly understood.
