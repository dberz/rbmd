The plant-based meat market is major—it’s projected to reach nearly $25 billion by 2030—fueled by growing concern about the environmental and health impact of red meat. Any form of industrial agriculture has a footprint but the beef industry is definitely bigger and worse. Even though plant-based “meats” are somewhat better for the environment, from a human health standpoint, I’ll take a pasture-raised, grass-fed organic burger over one of these chemical stews any day.
