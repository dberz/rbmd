Tally Health clocks me at 42 (DNA methylation). Heading into my mid-40s, I care less about candles on a cake than how old my body really is. Why? Because biological age predicts disease risk, functional health, and even mortality better than your chronological age ( Nature Scientific Reports , 2021). So which measure is “right”?
