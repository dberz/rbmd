Her HbA1c is 5.6. And nobody has connected the dots. Metabolic dysfunction is one of the most common — and most correctable — drivers of impaired fertility I see. And most conventional fertility workups don't even test for it. Here's what the research actually shows. Forward this protocol Insulin resistance silently disrupts ovulation, egg quality, and implantation — often before any cycle irregularity appears. The Metabolic Fertility Reset: Eat protein first at every meal (25–35g) to blunt glucose spikes Cut all added sugar to under 25g/day — including "healthy" sweeteners Do a 10-minute walk after your largest meal to lower postprandial glucose Get a 2-week CGM (continuous glucose monitor) to see your real-time blood sugar response Forward this to a friend who's been told her fertility workup is "normal" — but nobody checked her metabolic health.
